Our Services

Antles Specialists Program Management Services

We are one of the construction firms in the nation delivering Construction Management Services to both commercial and government entities. Our Construction Management Division initiates and maintains project schedules, financial status data and construction progress on complex projects across the Africa, having its headquarter in Capetown. We have demonstrated successful experience in commercial, industrial and governmental project management such as program management and financing

At Antles Specialists Program Management Company, our Construction Management Division is dedicated to delivering expert and professional service, tailored to your project-specific requirements.

Specific Services:

  • Construction Oversight

  • Review of submittals, safety plans and office drawings for contract compliance

  • Review of actual work performed to evaluate compliance with contract terms, contract schedule and make recommendations for contractors

  • General office consultant duties such as drafting change documents, preparing cost estimates, writing correspondence, reviewing contractor proposals, participating in various communications with contractors, customers, and internal staff, records establishment and maintenance

  • Directing quality insurance and safety meetings

  • Organizing work activities with customers and contractors.

  • Preparing daily quality insurance reports

  • Construction management for overall surveillance and management of construction projects or offices from conducting preconstruction conferences through final inspection and close-out.

  • Provide expert testimony or opinions or reports in connection with any of the above in support of change/claim/dispute administration

  • NEW - 3D visualization for new projects
